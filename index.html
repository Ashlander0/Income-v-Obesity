Correlations Discovered Between Income and Obesity

Obesity rates have a declining trend as income increases. This could be due to a number of factors, including access to healthcare, better equipped gyms, and personal trainers. Eating healthy foods may also correlate with income( might be more expencive), but this is not explored in this analysis. Higher income may also lead to a healthier general lifestyle, such as have time for a morning job before work.
